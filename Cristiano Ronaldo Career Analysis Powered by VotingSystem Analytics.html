Career Highlights & Defining Moments

2008: First Ballon d'Or & UCL Title

Ronaldo's breakthrough season at Manchester United saw him score 42 goals, win the Premier League, Champions League, and his first Ballon d'Or. His iconic headed goal in the UCL final against Chelsea capped a season that announced him as a global superstar.

2016: Euro 2016 Triumph

Despite injury in the final, Ronaldo led Portugal to their first major international trophy. His leadership throughout the tournament, including 3 goals and 3 assists, plus his touchline coaching in the final, cemented his legacy as Portugal's greatest ever player.

2014-2018: UCL Dominance

Ronaldo led Real Madrid to 4 Champions League titles in 5 years, including an unprecedented three-peat (2016-2018). He was top scorer in the competition for 6 consecutive seasons, scoring 15+ goals in three different campaigns—a feat no other player has achieved.

2021: International Records

Ronaldo broke Ali Daei's 109-goal international record that had stood since 2006. He extended it to 115+ goals and became the first men's player to reach 10 international hat-tricks. At 36, he also became the oldest player to score 5+ goals at a European Championship.
